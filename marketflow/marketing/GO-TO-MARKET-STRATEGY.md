# MarketFlow - Go-to-Market Strategy (US Launch)

## Executive Summary

**Product:** MarketFlow - E-Commerce Marketing Automation SaaS
**Target Market:** United States
**Primary Customer:** Shopify store owners ($10K-$5M monthly revenue)
**Launch Timeline:** 12 months from development start
**Initial Goal:** 1,000 paying customers in first year

---

## Market Analysis

### The Opportunity

**US E-commerce Market Size:**
- 2.1 million Shopify stores in the US
- 60% are actively advertising
- Average marketing spend: $500-$5,000/month

**Total Addressable Market (TAM):** $12.6B annually
**Serviceable Market (SAM):** $2.5B (small-medium businesses)
**Target Market (SOM):** $50M (first 3 years)

## Sales Strategy

### Self-Service Model (Primary)

**Flow:**
```
Website Visit → Free Trial (14 days) → Upgrade to Paid
```

**Optimization Points:**
1. Landing page conversion (target: 5%)
2. Trial activation (target: 70%)
3. Trial to paid (target: 15%)

### Inside Sales (For Scale+ Plans)

**When customer:**
- Needs custom features
- Has >$10K/month ad spend
- Is an agency

**Sales Process:**
1. Inbound inquiry or trial sign-up
2. Qualification call (15 min)
3. Product demo (30 min)
4. Proposal + negotiation
5. Onboarding

### Annual Plans

**Incentive:** 2 months free (17% discount)
**Goal:** 40% of customers on annual plans

## Customer Success Strategy

### Onboarding

**Week 1 - Getting Started:**
- Welcome email series (5 emails)
- Video tutorials
- Connect first integration
- In-app guidance

**Week 2 - First Campaign:**
- Live webinar: "Launch Your First Campaign"
- 1-on-1 onboarding call (Growth+ plans)
- Template recommendations

**Success Milestone:** First campaign live within 14 days

### Ongoing Support

| Plan | Support Level |
|------|---------------|
| Starter | Email (24h response) |
| Growth | Email + Chat (4h response) |
| Scale | Email + Chat + Phone (1h response) |
| Enterprise | Dedicated CSM |

### Reducing Churn

**Early Warning Signs:**
- No login in 7 days
- No campaigns running
- Support tickets unresolved
- Payment failed

**Interventions:**
- Automated re-engagement emails
- Proactive outreach from support
- Discount offers for at-risk customers
- Exit surveys for all churns

**Churn Target:** <5% monthly

## Risk Analysis

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| API changes from platforms | Medium | High | Monitor announcements, build flexibility |
| Scaling issues | Medium | Medium | Use proven cloud infrastructure |
| Security breach | Low | Very High | SOC 2 compliance, security audits |

### Market Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Strong competitor launches | Medium | High | Focus on differentiation, speed |
| Economic downturn | Medium | Medium | Focus on ROI messaging |
| Platform dependency (Shopify) | Low | High | Expand to other platforms |

### Operational Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Key team member leaves | Medium | High | Document everything, cross-train |
| Support overwhelmed | Medium | Medium | Build self-service resources |
| Cash flow issues | Medium | High | Conservative spending, runway planning |
